About Me

A little on my background:

I'm a front-end focused software engineer who loves building applications that people love to use! I think it's super important to take every opportunity to fully understand the needs of the user when designing interfaces, so that the simplest and most natural experiences can be created. I try to take a thoughtful approach to all my projects, continually assessing what are the best technologies, tools, and processes to solve a problem. I care most about causes that help empower individuals, like the personalization of education and healthcare.
